PyJaipur

PyJaipur is community driven group to promote Python in Jaipur. PyJaipur has been conducting monthly Meetups & workshops in Jaipur.We work together with other communities in particular, python and in general Open Source Software (OSS) related groups within Jaipur and the rest of the other commnumities to further expand the usage and increase users of OSS.

Our Goal

The goal of PyJaipur group, events, and conferences is to provide avenues for enthusiastic developers across various domains of real time use cases to share their experiences and techniques. PyJaipur is passionate about solving unique challenges with Python.
